E-commerce looks simple from the outside. It's not.

## Conversion is everything

A 1% improvement in conversion rate often beats a 10% increase in traffic. Focus on removing friction.

## Shipping is harder than it looks

Logistics will break you if you're not careful. Start simple, automate later.

## Customer service matters

One angry customer on social media can undo months of marketing. Respond fast, be human.
