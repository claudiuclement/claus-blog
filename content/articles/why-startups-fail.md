Most startups fail. Not because of bad luck, but because of avoidable mistakes.

## Building something nobody wants

The number one killer. Founders fall in love with their solution before validating the problem. Talk to users before writing code.

## Running out of money

Startups die when they run out of cash. Extend your runway by keeping costs low and focusing on revenue early.

## Wrong team

A mediocre idea with a great team beats a great idea with a mediocre team. Choose co-founders carefully.
